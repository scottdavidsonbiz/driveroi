Most companies need a RevOps consultant AND a BI/analytics consultant AND someone to do the actual technical work. We do all three. You get faster delivery, better integration, and one throat to choke."

### "This seems expensive compared to a freelancer"
**Response:** "Compare apples to apples. A Clay freelancer charges $3K-5K/month but can't build your BI infrastructure. **Response:** "Perfect - we augment, not replace. Your CRO sets strategy; we execute and build infrastructure. It's like having a full rev ops team without the hiring timeline and overhead. Most CROs are thrilled to have execution partners who actually understand strategy too."
