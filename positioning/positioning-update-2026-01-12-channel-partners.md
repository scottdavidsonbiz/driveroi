## üÜï Potential New Service: MDF Unlock Program

### What Is This?
**"We help you unlock $50K-$200K in vendor/carrier marketing development funds by creating the business case that justifies their investment."**

### How It Works
1. Client has vendor relationships (OEM, carrier, SaaS platform, etc.)
2. Vendor offers MDF (market development funds) but requires business case
3. We create: ICP definition, GTM approach, expected costs, expected results, tracking plan
4. Client submits to vendor, gets MDF approved
5. **We execute the campaign using the vendor's money** (not client's cash)

### Why This Is Genius
- Client gets $50K-$200K to spend on GTM (not out-of-pocket)
- Vendor gets proof their partner is driving demand
- We get paid to execute (could be $5K-$10K/month from MDF budget)
- **It's free money for everyone**

### Examples of Who Has MDF
- **Telecom carriers:** AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile (for channel partners)
- **OEMs:** Hardware manufacturers (routers, devices)
- **SaaS platforms:** Salesforce, HubSpot, Microsoft (for implementation partners)
- **Payment processors:** Stripe, Square (for ISVs)
