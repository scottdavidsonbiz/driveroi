## TRUSTAP ICP SUMMARY

### Target Personas:
1. **Co-founder/CEO** (Primary) - Early-stage marketplace founders feeling fraud/trust pain
2. **CTO/VP Engineering** (Technical Buyer) - Need payment integration that works
3. **VP Operations/COO** (Economic Buyer) - Drowning in manual refund processes

### Company Characteristics:
- **Type:** Peer-to-peer marketplaces (C2C, B2B, B2C)
- **Stage:** Post-seed to Series B
- **Revenue:** $100k - $5M GMV annually
- **Geography:** US, UK, Ireland, Europe
- **Pain Points:**
  - Payment processor limitations (manual refunds)
  - Fraud/scams destroying user trust
  - Can't scale with current payment solution
  - Need buyer protection to differentiate

### Key Buying Signals:
- Just raised funding (need to scale fast)
- Rapid user growth overwhelming systems
- Payment processor causing issues
- Fraud/scam complaints from users
- Expanding to new verticals/markets
- Manual processes eating operational time
