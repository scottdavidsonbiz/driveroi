# 25 MID-MARKET TARGET ACCOUNTS FOR BEEKEEPER

**Focus:** Mid-market companies (500-5,000 employees) with significant frontline workforces  

---

## BEEKEEPER'S MID-MARKET SWEET SPOT

### Recent Evidence They're Targeting Mid-Market:
According to a February 2024 press release, Beekeeper added **150+ new organizations** with explicit focus on **SMB and mid-market space**, including:
- **Polytainers** - Industrial packaging manufacturer
- **Rio Hotel & Casino Las Vegas** - Regional casino/hotel
- **Tree Top** - Food manufacturing cooperative
- **Ricola** - Herbal candy manufacturer (200-500 employees, $200M-$1B revenue)

### Mid-Market ICP Characteristics:
- **Employees:** 500-5,000 total, with 60%+ frontline workers
- **Locations:** 20-300 locations (regional, not national mega-chains)
- **Ownership:** Often family-owned, private equity-backed, or employee-owned
- **Growth stage:** Expanding rapidly (10-30% YoY growth)
- **Tech maturity:** Still using WhatsApp/SMS, no unified frontline platform
- **Decision maker access:** Easier to reach C-suite than at Fortune 500

## RESEARCH TIPS FOR MID-MARKET

### Finding Decision Makers:
1. **LinkedIn** - Much easier to find & connect with C-suite at mid-market
2. **Company website** - Often lists full leadership team
3. **Local business journals** - Profile new hires, promotions
4. **Industry conferences** - Easier to meet them in person

### Trigger Events to Watch:
1. **Recent IPO** (Dutch Bros, Portillo's) - Need enterprise infrastructure
2. **PE acquisition** (Zaxby's, Bojangles) - New owners demand metrics/efficiency
3. **Rapid expansion** (Orangetheory, Dutch Bros) - Culture breaking, need systems
4. **Unionization efforts** (Amy's Kitchen) - Need better worker communication
5. **Safety incidents** (Manufacturing/Construction) - Heightened compliance focus
6. **Seasonal hiring surge** (Resorts, Great Wolf) - Onboarding pain is acute

### Where to Find Company News:
- **Local business journals:** "Denver Business Journal", "Portland Business Journal", etc.
- **Industry publications:** *Restaurant Business*, *Convenience Store News*, *Club Industry*
- **Glassdoor reviews:** Search "communication" - if employees complain, timing is perfect!
- **LinkedIn job postings:** Hiring for "Internal Communications Manager"? Perfect trigger!
