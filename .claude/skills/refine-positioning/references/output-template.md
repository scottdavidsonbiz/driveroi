# Positioning Update Output Template

Use this template when creating positioning update documents.

## File Location

Save to: `positioning/positioning-update-YYYY-MM-DD.md`

## Template

```markdown
# Positioning Update: [Date]
**Based on:** [X] conversations from [date range]
**Analyzed:** [List of insight files]

## Executive Summary
[2-3 sentences: What did we learn? What changed?]

---

## Pain Point Patterns

### High Frequency (appeared in X+ conversations)
1. **[Pain point name]**
   - Appeared in: [X conversations]
   - Segments: [Which types of companies]
   - Quotes: "[Representative quote]"
   - Our solution: [How we address this]

### Medium Frequency
[Same format]

### Segment-Specific Pain Points
**SaaS Companies:**
- [Pain points unique to or dominant in SaaS]

**Non-SaaS Companies:**
- [Pain points unique to or dominant in non-SaaS]

---

## Buying Trigger Analysis

### Most Common Triggers
1. **[Trigger name]** - [X occurrences]
   - Example: "[Quote or scenario]"
   - Urgency level: [High/Medium/Low]
   - Segments where this appears: [List]

### Trigger Types by Frequency
- **Timing-based:** [Q1 planning, year-end, etc.]
- **Event-based:** [Just hired, just got funding, etc.]
- **Pain-based:** [Problem reached critical mass]
- **Transition-based:** [Scaling phase, new role, etc.]

---

## Market Segmentation

### Segment 1: [Name it descriptively]
- **Profile:** [Company size, type, stage]
- **Sample size:** [X conversations]
- **Key pain points:** [List]
- **Buying triggers:** [List]
- **Objections:** [List]
- **Value props that resonate:** [List]
- **Assessment:** [Is this a good target? Why/why not?]

### Segment 2: [Name]
[Same format]

### SaaS vs Non-SaaS Analysis
**SaaS Segment:**
- Conversations: [X]
- Common characteristics: [List]
- Saturation assessment: [Is this crowded? Evidence?]
- Opportunity: [Where's the white space in SaaS?]

**Non-SaaS Segment:**
- Conversations: [X]
- Common characteristics: [List]
- Differentiation: [How are their needs different?]
- Opportunity: [Is this less saturated? More opportunity?]

---

## Value Proposition Hierarchy

### What We Do Well (backed by conversations)
1. **[Value prop]**
   - Evidence: [Which conversations validated this]
   - Segments that care: [List]
   - Competitive advantage: [Why us?]

### What We Think We Do (not yet validated)
1. **[Claim we make]**
   - Need to test with: [Which segments]
   - Questions to ask: [How to validate]

---
```
