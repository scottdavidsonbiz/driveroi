# LinkedIn Metrics Tracking Guide

## Key Metrics by Priority

### Tier 1: Must Track (Core Business Metrics)
These directly impact revenue and business goals.

1. **LinkedIn → Discovery Calls**
   - Target: 3-5 calls/month (Month 1-3), 10+ calls/month (Month 4+)
   - How to track: Ask "How did you find me?" on every call

2. **Discovery Calls → Proposals**
   - Target: 30-50% conversion
   - How to track: Manual tracking in CRM/spreadsheet

3. **LinkedIn-Sourced Revenue**
   - Target: Track attribution
   - How to track: Tag deals with "LinkedIn" source

### Tier 2: Important (Growth Indicators)
These predict future business success.

4. **Profile Views per Week**
   - Target: 100+ (Month 1), 300+ (Month 3), 500+ (Month 6)
   - Where: LinkedIn Analytics → Profile Views

5. **Post Engagement Rate**
   - Formula: (Reactions + Comments + Shares) / Impressions
   - Target: 2-5% is good, 5%+ is excellent
   - Track by post to identify patterns

6. **Connection Acceptance Rate**
   - Formula: Accepted / Sent
   - Target: 40%+ (if engaging first), 20%+ (cold)
   - Indicates: Targeting quality, profile strength, message quality

7. **Quality Connections Added/Month**
   - Target: 50+ qualified connections/month
   - Quality > Quantity: ICP only

### Tier 3: Helpful (Optimization Signals)
These help optimize tactics.

8. **Post Impressions**
   - Track per post
   - Identify which topics get distribution

9. **Comments per Post**
   - Target: 5-10+ comments = good engagement
   - Quality of comments matters (not just "Great post!")

10. **DM Response Rate**
    - Formula: Responses / DMs sent
    - Target: 30%+ for warm prospects
    - Indicates: Relevance and value of outreach

## What Good Looks Like (Benchmarks)

### Month 1 Benchmarks
- **Posts:** 12-15 posts
- **Impressions per post:** 200-500 (organic, small network)
- **Engagement rate:** 1-3% (building audience)
- **Profile views:** 50-100/week
- **Connections:** 50+ qualified
- **Calls:** 1-3 from LinkedIn

### Month 3 Benchmarks
- **Posts:** 12-20 posts/month
- **Impressions per post:** 500-2000
- **Engagement rate:** 3-5%
- **Profile views:** 200-400/week
- **Connections:** 100-150 total qualified
- **Calls:** 5-10 from LinkedIn

### Month 6 Benchmarks
- **Posts:** 15-20 posts/month (if consistent)
- **Impressions per post:** 1000-5000
- **Engagement rate:** 4-7%
- **Profile views:** 400-800/week
- **Connections:** 250-400 total qualified
- **Calls:** 10-15 from LinkedIn
- **Revenue:** First LinkedIn-sourced deals

## How to Access LinkedIn Analytics

**For Individual Posts:**
1. Click on post
2. View analytics (impressions, reactions, comments, shares)
3. See who viewed/engaged

**For Profile:**
1. Go to your profile
2. View analytics/dashboard
3. See profile views, search appearances, post impressions

**Creator Mode Analytics:**
1. Turn on Creator Mode
2. Access dashboard
3. See follower growth, content performance, trending topics

## Content Performance Analysis

### Questions to Ask Weekly:

**What's working?**
- Which content format got highest engagement?
- Which topic/pillar performed best?
- What time of day works best?
- Which posts got saved/shared?

**What's not working?**
- Which posts had lowest engagement?
- What topics fall flat?
- What formats to avoid?

**Patterns to identify:**
- Do tactical posts outperform stories?
- Do lists perform better than paragraphs?
- Do questions boost comments?
- Does tagging help or hurt?

### Action Based on Data:

**If engagement is low:**
- Improve hooks (first line)
- Make content more specific/actionable
- Engage more before posting
- Post at different times

**If impressions are low:**
- Engage with others more (pre and post publishing)
- Use relevant hashtags (2-3 max)
- Tag relevant people/companies
- Post when audience is online

**If profile views are low but posts perform well:**
- Check if profile is optimized
- Ensure headline is compelling
- Add clear CTA in posts

## Connection Quality Metrics

**Good signals:**
- They comment on your posts
- They accept quickly
- They engage after connecting
- They respond to value-first DMs
- They match ICP criteria

**Bad signals:**
- Never engage with content
- Generic profile (potential spam)
- Outside ICP
- No mutual connections
- Immediately pitch you something

**Track conversion:**
| Connection Quality | % of Connections | % That Become Calls | % That Close |
|-------------------|------------------|---------------------|--------------|
| Hot (engaged first) | 30% | 15% | 5% |
| Warm (mutual connections) | 50% | 5% | 2% |
| Cold (no engagement) | 20% | 1% | 0.5% |

Adjust targeting based on data.

## Red Flags to Watch For

**Declining engagement rate:**
- Content getting stale?
- Not engaging enough with others?
- Posting too frequently or infrequently?

**Connections not converting:**
- Wrong ICP targeting?
- Profile not optimized?
- Not providing enough value?

**High views but low business results:**
- Content is entertaining but not attracting ICP?
- No clear CTA or next step?
- Not moving conversations off-platform?
